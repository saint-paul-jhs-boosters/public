# Saint Paul Johnson High School Football Boosters, Inc.

Supporting student-athletes at Saint Paul Johnson High School through community fundraising, volunteerism, and engagement.

---

## About Us

We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting the football program at Saint Paul Johnson High School. Our mission is to enhance student-athlete development and educational opportunities through community involvement.

**Legal Name:** Saint Paul Johnson High School Football Boosters, Inc.
**Operating Name:** StP JHS FB Boosters
