## Summary

The founding members of Saint Paul Johnson High School Football Boosters, Inc. held an introductory session on June 3, 2026. This was an informal organizational meeting — no formal votes were taken. A formal board meeting will be scheduled to elect officers, confirm board membership, and ratify the Articles of Incorporation and Bylaws.

---

## Topics Covered

### Introductions
Attendees introduced themselves and shared their connection to Saint Paul Johnson High School and the football program.

### Organization Overview
The group reviewed proposed roles, organizational structure, and what would be needed to get the organization formally operational.

### Road Ahead
The group discussed major milestones ahead:
- Filing Articles of Incorporation with the Minnesota Secretary of State
- Obtaining an EIN from the IRS
- Applying for 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status
- Opening a nonprofit bank account
- Building a website and membership program
- Planning the first fundraising events

### Filing Costs
Eric Moberg committed to personally sponsoring all initial filing costs on behalf of the organization, including state incorporation fees, IRS EIN application, and 501(c)(3) application fee.

### Meeting Transcription
Eric Moberg committed to establishing a transcription service for all future board and member meetings to ensure accurate and complete records.

### Transparency Commitment
The group established a founding principle of **full transparency**:
- Nothing is hidden from members or the community
- Bank statements reviewed at every board meeting
- Financial summaries published publicly after every meeting
- All governance documents publicly available
