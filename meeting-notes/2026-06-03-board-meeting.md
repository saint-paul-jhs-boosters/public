## Summary

The founding board of Saint Paul Johnson High School Football Boosters, Inc. held its first meeting on June 3, 2026. The meeting served as an introduction for board members and an initial planning session to outline the road ahead.

---

## Topics Covered

### Introductions
Board members introduced themselves and shared their connection to Saint Paul Johnson High School and the football program.

### Organization Structure
The board reviewed the organization's structure, officer roles, and each member's responsibilities going forward.

### Road Ahead
The board discussed the major milestones needed to get the organization operational:
- Filing Articles of Incorporation with the Minnesota Secretary of State
- Obtaining an EIN from the IRS
- Applying for 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status
- Opening a nonprofit bank account
- Building a website and membership program
- Planning the first fundraising events

### Filing Costs
Eric Moberg (Secretary) committed to personally sponsoring all initial filing costs on behalf of the organization, including state incorporation fees, IRS EIN application, and 501(c)(3) application fee.

### Meeting Transcription
Eric Moberg committed to establishing a transcription service for all future board and member meetings to ensure accurate and complete records.

### Transparency Commitment
The board established a founding principle of **full transparency**:
- Nothing is hidden from members or the community
- Bank statements reviewed at every board meeting
- Financial summaries published publicly after every meeting
- All governance documents publicly available

This commitment to transparency is a core value of the organization and will guide all future decisions.
